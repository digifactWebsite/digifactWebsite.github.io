5 bài học từ hành trình TEC Startup Program

Những insight quý giá cho các startup khác muốn tham gia TEC. Từ chuẩn bị hồ sơ đến presentation skills và team building.

Sau 3 tháng intense nhưng rewarding trong TEC Startup Program 2025, team DigiFact muốn share những lessons learned có thể valuable cho các startup khác đang consider apply hoặc đã participate trong similar programs. Đây không phải chỉ là business insights, mà còn về personal growth, team dynamics, và strategic thinking.

1

Customer Validation Trumps Perfect Product

Biggest misconception của chúng tôi initially là thinking rằng cần perfect product trước khi talk to customers. TEC program taught us rằng customer feedback là most valuable input for product development.

Key Insight

Customers don't buy features, they buy solutions to their problems. Focus on problem-solution fit trước product-market fit.

Trong giai đoạn đầu, DigiFact có comprehensive feature set nhưng unclear value proposition. Sau customer discovery interviews, chúng tôi realize rằng customers care more về ROI và ease of implementation rather than technical sophistication.

Practical Applications

  • Start customer interviews từ day 1, không wait for perfect MVP
  • Ask about their current pain points, not về features they want
  • Validate willingness to pay trước khi build expensive features
  • Use customer language trong marketing materials, not technical jargon

Common Mistake

Don't ask customers "Would you use this feature?" mà ask "How are you solving this problem today?" và "What would make you switch from current solution?"

2

Business Model Canvas là Living Document

Trước TEC, chúng tôi treat business model như static plan. Program taught us rằng business model should evolve based on learnings và market feedback. Canvas không phải one-time exercise mà continuous refinement process.

Key Insight

Every customer conversation, every market research, every partnership discussion should potentially update your business model canvas.

DigiFact đã iterate business model 3 lần during TEC program:

  • Version 1: Freemium model with advertising revenue
  • Version 2: Pure SaaS subscription model
  • Version 3: Tiered SaaS with professional services add-on

Iteration Best Practices

  • Review canvas weekly với team để incorporate new learnings
  • Test assumptions với smallest viable experiments
  • Document reasoning behind each pivot để avoid circular thinking
  • Validate new model với existing customers trước major changes
3

Financial Modeling Beyond Excel Spreadsheet

Initial financial model của DigiFact was basically revenue projections trong Excel. TEC program showed us importance của unit economics, customer acquisition cost (CAC), lifetime value (LTV), và cash flow management for sustainable growth.

Key Insight

Financial model should tell story của how your business will scale profitably, not just optimistic revenue numbers.

Game-changing metrics chúng tôi learned to track:

  • LTV:CAC Ratio: Should be 3:1 or higher for healthy business
  • Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) growth: Predictable revenue indicator
  • Churn rate: Customer retention và satisfaction measure
  • Net Revenue Retention (NRR): Expansion revenue từ existing customers
  • Cash runway: How long company can operate với current cash

Financial Planning Tips

  • Build 3 scenarios: conservative, realistic, optimistic
  • Focus on unit economics trước worry về scale
  • Track leading indicators (trials, demos) not just lagging (revenue)
  • Plan cash flow monthly, not annually
  • Understand correlation between marketing spend và customer acquisition
4

Storytelling = Competitive Advantage

Technical founders often underestimate power của compelling storytelling. TEC program emphasized rằng how you communicate vision, progress, và challenges can differentiate you từ competitors với similar products.

Key Insight

People invest in stories they believe in, not just products they understand. Your narrative should connect emotionally với audience.

DigiFact story evolution:

  • Before: "We build manufacturing software"
  • After: "We help family manufacturers compete với Industry 4.0 giants by democratizing smart factory technology"

Storytelling Framework

  • Hero: Your customer (not your product)
  • Conflict: Pain points và challenges they face
  • Resolution: How your solution enables their success
  • Transformation: Vision của better future state

Balance Needed

Compelling story must be grounded in facts. Avoid over-promising or creating unrealistic expectations. Authenticity beats hype every time.

5

Network Effect Beyond Social Media

TEC program's greatest value wasn't just training content, but network access. Connections với mentors, other startups, potential customers, và investors create compound benefits over time.

Key Insight

Your network is your net worth, but more importantly, your network accelerates your learning curve và opens doors bạn didn't know existed.

Unexpected benefits từ TEC network:

  • Customer referrals: 2 paying customers came từ other startups trong cohort
  • Partnership opportunities: Integration possibilities với complementary solutions
  • Talent acquisition: Recommended developers và designers từ network
  • Investor warm introductions: Mentor connections to seed funds
  • Knowledge sharing: Real-time advice on challenges from experienced founders

Networking Best Practices

  • Give before you ask - share knowledge, make introductions
  • Follow up consistently nhưng don't spam
  • Be specific trong requests - "Can you introduce me to X for Y reason?"
  • Maintain relationships beyond immediate needs
  • Join relevant communities và contribute valuable content

Advice for Future TEC Applicants

Nếu bạn đang consider apply for TEC Startup Program hoặc similar accelerator program:

Application Tips

  • Demonstrate traction: Show customer validation, revenue, or strong user engagement
  • Clear problem statement: Articulate specific pain point you're solving
  • Scalable solution: Explain how your approach can grow beyond initial market
  • Committed team: Show dedication và complementary skills
  • Realistic projections: Ambitious but achievable goals

Time Management Reality

TEC program is intensive. Expect 40+ hours/week commitment. Make sure your team có bandwidth để fully participate while maintaining business operations.

Final Reflection

TEC Startup Program 2025 has been transformational journey for DigiFact - not just in terms of business growth, nhưng personal development của entire team. The combination of structured learning, mentorship, networking, và peer support created environment conducive to rapid iteration và improvement.

Most importantly, program reinforced belief rằng startup success isn't just về great product or innovative technology. It's about understanding customers deeply, building sustainable business model, communicating vision effectively, và leveraging community for accelerated growth.

For any startup considering similar program: investment in structured learning và mentorship pays compound returns. The insights, connections, và confidence gained during these intensive months will benefit your venture for years to come.
